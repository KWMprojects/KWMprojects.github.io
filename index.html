About

A few years ago, I was introduced to LWJGL for Java game development by a friend and was excited by the endless possibilities. While reading ‘How to Think Like a Computer Scientist’ in Java, I became hooked on the process of completing the book’s challenges, learning new languages and implementing algorithms. Nothing beats the feeling of finally solving a tough problem. As a student at Code Platoon, I’m learning to be a full stack developer and plan to bring my acquired skills to a new career in tech. I have witnessed the vital impact that technology can have for combat, training and day-to-day operations. Software development is the tempo and limitless innovation I crave. I’m new to the tech industry and I’m ready to go all-in. I’m actively seeking a new career in software development and would like to discuss new opportunities.
